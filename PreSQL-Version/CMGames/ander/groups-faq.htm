What’s the point of Groups anyway?

Groups have two purposes, the first is as a notification list for events.  The second is to screen admittance to events.

My friends told me they joined my Group, why can’t I see them?

If a group is a closed group pending memberships are not listed in the usual members list, but are visible only to the President and Senior Officers in the ‘adjust ranks’ page.  If you are the president or have been made a Senior Officer by the President, go to the group page, click on adjust ranks and set your friend to the appropriate rank.

I’m a senior officer, How do I give other people the authority to create games, or approve members?

Follow the instructions for making members visible above.

I’m the president – how do I suspend people or make them senior officers?

This is also done on the adjust rank page.

What are these ranks anyway?

Ranks are a way of controlling group permissions.  Normal members, for instance, can not send out group notices, only officers may do this.  Junior Officers can send out notices, but can not give others the power to send out notices.  Seniors Officers can appoint and remove Junior Officers, in addition to creating official games.

I’ve been suspended – what does this mean?

It is as though you are not a member, but with one difference, you can not simply reapply.  Only the President may suspend or lift a suspension – get in contact with him or her.

I received a notice of a game being set up by my group – I don’t see this game, how do I join it?

Go the group page, and click on Official Events.  A list of all official games, past, present and future appears.  Click on the (present or future) game you wish to join, and click on the Join link.

I’m an officer in a group – how do I create an official game and send out a notice?

All official games have notices sent out.  To create a game go the group page, click on Official Games, then click create official game.  The Category and game must be the official game of the group, but fill in the rest of the details in the normal way.  You may choose whether to restrict admission to group members or not, by selecting either closed or open game.

I want to create a closed game, but not of the official group game, can I do this?

Yes.  Go to the group page and click on Closed Games.  Click create closed game, and set up your game in the usual way.  The default is still to create an open event, but you can confirm you want a closed event by simply dropping the menu bar saying open and selecting closed.

If I’m not an officer of a group – but I am a member, can I still create a closed game?

Yes.  Follow the instructions above.

How do I join a closed game?

Go to the group page and click Closed Events.  Click on the event and click Join.

I feel someone is abusing their notice privileges – can I do anything about this?

If the person is a junior officer, any senior officer or the president can demote them to regular membership.  A senior officer can be demoted by the President.  If the President is abusing this privilege however, your best option is to leave the group.
